APPLY

FOR UC BERKELEY STUDENTS

For students interested in working on a project or teaching data science: You can apply to our education team, projects team, or both. There is only one application. Good luck!

FOR HIGH SCHOOL STUDENTS

For high school students interested in learning about data science: Apply to be a part of our 9-week program hosted at UC Berkeley where you will learn what data science is, how you can apply it to any industry you’re interested in, and see it in action!

Email bigdata.berkeley@gmail.com with any questions.
