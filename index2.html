BIG DATA AT BERKELEY

We are a student organization that strives to promote data science fundamentals while impacting our community through educational workshops and data analytics projects.

OUR VISION

We are a group of students who came into college with no prior introduction to the new and rapidly evolving field of data science. After being exposed to the innovative curriculum at UC Berkeley, completing a few industry-level projects, and working as members of the Data 8 course staff, we realized the impact this field can have on our world and its importance in education.
